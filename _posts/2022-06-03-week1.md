I started the summer project with a stakeholder meeting, put on by Dr. Vinod Namboodiri. It was at the campus of Wichita State University. I was first able to use the app to navigate to his lab, through an unfamiliar building. It was an excellent demonstration that demonstrated the capabilities of his wayfinding technology.

The 2nd half of the meeting was with a roomful of mostly business  professionals that were invited to contribute in the vision for the future of the project. Dr. Namboodiri did not get into the technical details of the application, database, or software using the project. However he did explain the Bluetooth beacon technology that is providing the location information used in the wayfinding.

I finished the rest of the week reading the published papers directly related to the CITYGUIDE app.
