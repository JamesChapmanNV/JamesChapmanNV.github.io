I started the summer with the opportunity to attend a stakeholder meeting, for the CITYGUIDE project. Dr. Vinod Namboodiri hosted the meeting, with approximately 25 guests. Most of the guests were representatives of organizations in Wichita that could benefit from CITYGUIDE. It was on campus at Wichita State University. 

First, I was able to use and demonstrate the app to navigate through an unfamiliar building, and into Dr. Namboodiri’s ACCESS laboratory. It was an excellent demonstration that showcased the capabilities of his wayfinding technology.

The 2nd half of the meeting was a presentation by Dr. Namboodiri and his students that have been working on the different components of the application. He did not get into the technical details of the application, database, or software specifics of the project. However he did explain the Bluetooth beacon technology that is providing the location information used in the wayfinding.

I finished the rest of the week reading the published papers directly related to the CITYGUIDE app.
