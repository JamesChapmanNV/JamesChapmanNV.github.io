During week 7, I had COVID for the first time and, as a quadriplegic, I was worried about the effects that could come from having it. However, I just took it slow and now I am already recovering and ready to move on from it. 
I spent this week investigating further into the state of indoor mapping. Not just in literature but the need for indoor mapping in the disabled community. A large part of the focus of this summer project is the applications of indoor mapping in the disability community.  Addressing the needs of the very diverse disabled community is very important.
I have been searching through so much different material, and I have collected a small stockpile of papers and websites. Every once in a while I search for different papers expand the scope of the research.
